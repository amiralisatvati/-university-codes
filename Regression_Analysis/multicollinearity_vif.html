Multicollinearity Analysis: VIF and Tolerance

Variance Inflation Factor (VIF)

VIF measures how much the variance of a regression coefficient increases due to multicollinearity.

VIF Interpretation

  • VIF = 1: No correlation
  • VIF 1-5: Moderate correlation
  • VIF 5-10: High correlation
  • VIF > 10: Very high correlation (problematic)
